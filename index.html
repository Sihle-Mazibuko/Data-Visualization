Style guide

Design goal

This website aims to first provide accurate information that users can understand and second give it a galactic or out-of-space feel.

Colour choices

The previously mentioned goal of giving this website a galactic feel is achieved through the colour choices used in the site. The website is predominantly black and white, mimicking how space looks like, a black void with white stars. The website is specifically about astronomy pictures and such pictures contain various unique colours.Implementing all these colours will not be ideal for viewers of the sight as it makes things look unorganized however there are subtle details (such as the animated footer) that show the website has variety in the choice of colours.

Font choice

A sans-serif font, namely Helvetica is used in the entire website. This is due to sans-serifs being considered one of the easiest fonts to read and they induce the least eye strain on the reader.

Layout choices

Every plain text in the website has been placed in the middle to keep the viewers eyes locked into one area of the site at all times

Other choices

Buttons: The buttons found on the website are included are found in the header and each serves to take the user to a specific section in the website or change units.

Dropdowns: When scrolling down the website the header becomes a sticky drop-down that is always present at the top. On smaller screens, the navigation buttons and their text are hidden and only the dropdown toggle is visible.

Icons: The only icons present on the website are the toggle and the drop-down bar.

Minimalistic interface elements give a simple and minimalistic aesthetic to the website.

Animations: The background, website logo and footer were all given animations to give the website a feeling of movement. The downside to this is that it is straining on the user as the white text and white stars moving can cause the eyes to not fully focus on one thing however this has been dealt with by giving the stars a dark colour, which also gives them a distant look.
